**AI Job Market Insights Dashboard**
This project visualizes global trends in AI job postings using **Tableau**, based on key factors like job title, experience level, company size, industry,
location, and required skills.The dashboard offers insights into salary progression, hiring demand, company behavior, and seasonal trends across the AI sector.

**Data Overview**

The dataset includes fields like:

- "job_title"
- "salary_usd
- "experience_level" (Entry, Mid, Senior, Expert)
- "company_size" (Small, Medium, Large)
- "required_skills" (Python, NLP, Azure, etc.)
- "industry" and "location" fields
- "remote_ratio", "posting_date"

The data was cleaned, transformed, and visualized in Tableau using calculated fields, parameters, and filters.

**Dashboard 1**: AI Job Market Insights

- Average Salary:$115,349  
- Total Job Listings: 15,000  
- Remote Job Share: 32.8%  
- Average Benefits Score: 7.5  
- Average Experience Required: 6.25 years
  
**Dashboard 2**: Salary and Skills Trends

- Top-paying roles: AI Architect, AI Consultant, Principal Data Scientist
- Most in-demand skills: AWS, Azure, Git, Docker, MLOps, NLP
- Average salary increases with company size
- Job postings spread across all experience levels

**Key Findings**

- Experts earn nearly 3x more than entry-level roles.
- Large companies offer higher salaries, but small firms hire just as much.
- Remote jobs are common but slightly lower-paid on average.
- Skills in cloud computing and AI infrastructure drive higher salaries.
- Job openings are balanced across experience levels.
  
**How to Use**

1. Clone the repository or download the `.twbx` file.
2. Open it in **Tableau Desktop** (version 2021.4 or newer recommended).
3. Interact with filters (Job Title, Location, Experience Level, etc.).
4. View dynamic charts and drill into segments of interest.
